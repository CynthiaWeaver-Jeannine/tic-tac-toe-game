Game Info

Starting: The starting player is chosen at random in each new game.

Moves: You play as X against Max as O.

Scoreboard: The scoreboard keeps track of your wins, Max's wins, and draws. Reset at any time.

To Win: Connect 3 of your symbols in a row, a column, or a diagonal.

Pro Tip: Be strategic! Max can be quite the opponent. Have fun!
